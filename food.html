Delicious Italian Food

Italian food is famous all over the world. It is simple, tasty, and full of flavor. People love it!

Pizza

Pizza is round, hot, and delicious. It has cheese, tomato sauce, and many toppings. Italians eat pizza with friends and family.

Pasta

Pasta is a very popular dish in Italy. There are many kinds: spaghetti, penne, lasagna. It is often served with tomato or cheese sauce.

Gelato

Gelato is Italian ice cream. It is cold, sweet, and comes in many flavors like chocolate, vanilla, and strawberry. People eat it in summer.
