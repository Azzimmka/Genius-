Art & History of Italy

Italy has a long and rich history. It is famous for beautiful art, old buildings, and smart people who changed the world.

The Colosseum

The Colosseum is in Rome. It is a very old building where people watched shows and fights. Today, it is a famous place for visitors.

Leonardo da Vinci

Leonardo was a great artist and inventor. He painted the Mona Lisa and made many smart ideas. He lived in Italy 500 years ago.

Renaissance

The Renaissance was a time of art and new thinking in Italy. People made great paintings, buildings, and books during this time.
